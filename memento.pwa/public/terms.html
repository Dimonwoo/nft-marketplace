Creating Geo-Located Photos

The user will also have the opportunity to attach BSV (Bitcoin) to their Relic. When a Relica user’s GPS matches the placement of the Relic location chosen by the user, the user will be notified that they have ‘found’ the Relic by a notification and an animated image of confetti will appear over the image which has been found.

Relica may create also create Relics to be left in certain places for users to locate and benefit from. If the Photo has BSV – Bitcoin attached, the first finder of the Photo will receive that amount of Bitcoin into their private wallet.

Full details on how to 'Create a Relic' function can be found on the platform. Should you have any questions about how to find a geo-located Relic, or any other questions, please get in touch with us at contact@Relica.world.
